Privacy Policy

I don't store your data.

Anything you do on Launch is saved to your browser. All files and folders you make are stored in LocalStorage, within your browser itself.

There is no database on the server for your data to be stored on.

Note: If you want to hide the "Privacy" link at the bottom of launch, use the command: launch-hide-privacy (Or to show it again: launch-show-privacy )
